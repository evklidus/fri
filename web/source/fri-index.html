Methodology

The Formula

Fair, transparent, and position-neutral. Every component is publicly auditable.

FRI = (P × 0.35) + (S × 0.20) + (F × 0.20) + (M × 0.15) + (C × 0.10) All components normalized to 0–100 scale · Updated every 24 hours · Live news events trigger immediate recalculation

🏟️ Field Performance

Weight: 35%

Stats normalized by position and league average. GK measured by saves, DEF by tackles/interceptions, MID by key passes, FWD by goals per 90. Age-adjusted for veterans.

📱 Social Influence

Weight: 20%

Log-scaled follower count across Instagram, X, TikTok. Weighted by engagement rate and 30-day follower growth. Prevents megastars from crowding out everyone else.

🗳️ Fan Sentiment

Weight: 20%

Rolling weekly polls + per-match fan ratings. Verified voters only (no bots). Blended 60/40 between structured polls and post-match crowd ratings.

📰 Media Score

Weight: 15%

NLP sentiment analysis on news articles from 200+ outlets. Positive coverage raises score, negative stories lower it. Weighted by media outlet authority ranking.

⚖️ Character Index

Weight: 10%

Yellow/red cards, confirmed scandals, fan conflicts, referee disputes subtract points. Charity work and fair play awards add. Older incidents decay 20% weight per year.
