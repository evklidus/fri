Methodology

The Formula

Fair, transparent, and position-neutral. Every component is publicly auditable.

FRI = (P × 0.40) + (S × 0.25) + (M × 0.20) + (C × 0.15) All components normalized to 0–100 scale · Updated every 24 hours · Live news events trigger immediate recalculation

🏟️ Field Performance

Weight: 40%

Stats normalized by position and league average. GK measured by saves, DEF by tackles/interceptions, MID by key passes, FWD by goals per 90. Blended 60/40 with a career baseline so stars don't crater during an off year.

📱 Social Influence

Weight: 25%

Log-scaled follower count across Instagram, X, TikTok. Weighted by engagement rate and 30-day follower growth. Prevents megastars from crowding out everyone else.

📰 Media Score

Weight: 20%

NLP sentiment analysis on news articles from 200+ outlets. Positive coverage raises score, negative stories lower it. Weighted by media outlet authority ranking.

⚖️ Character Index

Weight: 15%

Yellow/red cards, scandals, referee disputes subtract points. Charity work, fair play awards, captaincy add. Each event runs through community voting (see "Vote on Events") so obvious cases auto-apply and grey-area ones are validated by fans.
